BarryBot, named after my pet fish, is a basic chatbot that sends different responses to users based on what they say. He also has basic commands such as flipping a coin, rolling a dice, and kicking users. He also a chance of kicking half the members of a server with the command "Barry snap" in honor of Avengers Infinity War.

Gif Charades

Gif Charades is a simple game where a user types "/gc play" and is then presented with a subject such as "movie" a word count, and a series of gifs. The user then must tell the bot what the title of the subject is. While BarryBot is more for personal use, Gif Charades is on the official Discord bot store.
